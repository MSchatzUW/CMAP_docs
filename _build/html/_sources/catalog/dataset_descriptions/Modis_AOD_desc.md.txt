The MODIS AOD dataset in CMAP is a monthly averaged 1x1 degree gridded dataset. This dataset was generated from NASA's MODIS Aqua satellite.
Aerosol Optical Depth is a measurement of the optical scattering caused by airborne atmospheric particles.
Sources of these particles include: volcanic ash, wildfire smoke, windblown sand and dust.
The **MODIS MYD08_M3** dataset is a satellite derived level-3 gridded product. Monthly AOD values are averaged over 1x1 degree pixels.
